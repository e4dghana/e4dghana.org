Learning from Ghana's 2025 Grain Glut

Rufai Issifu

Ghana's 2025 bumper harvest exposed critical weaknesses in surplus management. A GHC 300m government response stabilised prices, but structural reforms — from commodity exchanges to agro-processing incentives and export coordination — are urgently needed.
