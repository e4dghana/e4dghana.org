Featured Projects

Youth-led initiatives creating measurable impact across Northern Ghana.

Every project E4D Ghana delivers is designed with and for the communities it serves — locally rooted, evidence-informed, and led by young people.

Digital Skills for Youth

Training 500+ young people in coding, digital literacy, and online entrepreneurship to bridge the digital divide across Northern Ghana. The programme equips youth with practical technology skills — from basic computer literacy to web development and digital marketing — creating pathways to employment and self-employment in the modern economy.

Youth Peace Ambassadors

Building bridges through inter-community dialogue, mediation training, and civic engagement among young leaders in the North East Region. Youth Peace Ambassadors are trained in structured conflict resolution and serve as local mediators in their communities — reducing tension and building lasting social cohesion.

North East Region

Campus2Career (C2C)

Connecting university students and fresh graduates with professional mentors, scholarship support, internship pathways, and peer networks to accelerate career readiness across Northern Ghana. C2C operates through a vibrant WhatsApp community, regular webinars, and one-on-one mentorship matching.

Multi-campus, Northern Ghana

Community-driven climate adaptation in Karaga District. Youth-led agroforestry, soil conservation, and natural resource management to improve food security and ecological sustainability among smallholder farming communities.
