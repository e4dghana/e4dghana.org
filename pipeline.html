Seeking Partners 01
YACA

Youth Accelerate Climate Action

A youth-led climate education and advocacy initiative aimed at strengthening climate literacy and community resilience in rural Northern Ghana. The project will train young people as Climate Action Ambassadors to promote climate-smart and sustainable agricultural practices among farmers in selected communities of the Kumbungu District.

Through community dialogues, storytelling sessions, training workshops, and social media advocacy, YACA seeks to empower youth leadership in climate action while supporting farmers to better adapt to climate change and environmental challenges.

Seeking Partners 02
RESTORE–Ghana · Resilient Engagement for Social Trust, Opportunity, Reconciliation & Empowerment

RESTORE–Ghana

A youth-led peace and climate resilience initiative that brings together farmers, Fulbe herders, youth, women, and community leaders in conflict-affected communities in the Mion, Karaga and Gushegu districts, Northern Region.

Through intergenerational storytelling, restorative healing circles, and community sports, the project creates safe spaces for dialogue, healing, and connection — reducing farmer–herder tensions and strengthening social cohesion.

Seeking Partners 03
RESILIENT–Ghana

RESILIENT–Ghana

A youth-led climate justice and peacebuilding initiative that will address climate-driven tensions between farmers and Fulbe herders in selected districts in Northern Ghana.

The project will train Climate Justice Ambassadors and mobilise communities through intergenerational dialogues, restorative healing circles, and community sports to promote climate literacy, sustainable natural resource use, and peaceful coexistence.

By empowering youth and strengthening collaboration among farmers, herders, and community leaders, RESILIENT–Ghana will help build resilient communities, reduce resource-based conflicts, and advance climate adaptation.
